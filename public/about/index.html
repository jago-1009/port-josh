Joshua Garwood was born and raised in Indianapolis, IN. and has been pursuing music as a clarinetist since the impressionable age of 12 years old. He has had the opportunity to work directly with students in high schools all over central Indiana through private lessons, summer camps, and as a mentor. He has worked and studied with many different clarinetists including Rajesh Soodeen, Elizabeth Crawford, Claire Grellier, Michele Gingras, and Elisha Willinger. He is currently working on his bachelor's degree in Music Education at Ball State University. In addition to this, he is also involved numerous ensembles including the Ball State Wind Ensemble, Ball State Symphony Orchestra, and the Orchestra Indiana Woodwind Quintet.
